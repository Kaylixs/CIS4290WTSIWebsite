Wireless Telecommunication Symposium

04-22 April 2020, San Francisco, USA

What is the Latest in Global Wireless Communications?

Exchange information on advances in mobile communications and wireless networking technology, management, policy, applications, and security in New York City, New York, USA with leaders and experts from industry, governmental agencies, and universities around the world at the 18th annual Wireless Telecommunications Symposium.

WTS 2019 will focus on Global Wireless Communications: Now and in the Future. Planned highlights of WTS 2019 include:

  • Addresses and presentations by some of the most respected executives and researchers in the wireless communications industry
  • Panel discussions
  • Tutorials and workshops
  • Presentations of accepted academic and practitioner research papers; a poster paper session; a doctoral students session
  • Off-site social events and tours
